About Me

Designing with Purpose

I'm Alexandra Chen, a multidisciplinary graphic designer with a passion for creating meaningful visual experiences that connect brands with their audiences.

With over a decade of experience working with startups and Fortune 500 companies, I bring a unique blend of strategic thinking and creative execution to every project. My approach combines minimalist aesthetics with bold, impactful design choices.
