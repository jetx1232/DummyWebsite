The Brief

Understanding the Challenge

Ethereal Studio approached us with a clear mission: transform their dated brand into a sophisticated identity that reflects their evolution into a premium creative agency.

The Challenge

The existing brand identity was:

  • Visually outdated with a generic corporate feel
  • Inconsistent across different touchpoints
  • Not reflecting their premium positioning
  • Failing to attract high-value clients

The Solution

We delivered a complete brand transformation:

  • Bold new visual identity with luxury positioning
  • Cohesive brand system across all touchpoints
  • Premium color palette with deep purples and gold
  • Modern typography that commands attention
